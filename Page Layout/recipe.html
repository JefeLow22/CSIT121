Lemon Pepper Wings

Jeff Lowery's "wings so good make you want to smack yo mama"

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup avocado oil
  • 5 lb chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon pepper seasoning
  • 2 tabespoons of dry ranch seasoning
  • 1 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons of finely diced garlic cloves
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1 pinch of old bay seasoning

Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F.
  2. Mix chicken wings,oil, and garlic cloves. Place wings on a wire rack set on a baking sheet, Make sure not to crowd wings. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.
  3. Remove wings from the oven. Mix melted butter,lemon-pepper seasoning,dry ranch seasoning, cayenne pepper, and old bay seasoning and brush a coat onto wings.
  4. Allow to cool for a min or two, serve and enjoy.
  5. Pair with your favorite sides. Ie: fries, baked potato, or a small salad

This is my favorite recipe to make because I always get a kick out of seeing my family or friends take their first bite from my wings.
