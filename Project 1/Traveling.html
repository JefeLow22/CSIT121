Go Fourth, and seek your paradise

I travel simply for the idea of adventurous freedom. The freedom to go to different places,and explore cultures I never knew existed. The opportunity to become friends with strangers I'll never forget, try foods that'll turn my taste buds upside down, and capture experiences that'll stay with me for a lifetime. I've been to over 15 different countries, and I don't plan on stopping anytime soon. We only go through this life once, enjoy the journey!
