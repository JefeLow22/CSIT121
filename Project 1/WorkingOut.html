Put in the work now, enjoy the results later

Working out for me is a form of meditation. It offers me peace and tranquility in exchange for sweat and hardwork. I block out everything going on around me, and completely focus on the task at hand. The natural body high I receive after a serious workout is a testament to how the body and mind synchronize to achieve something great.
